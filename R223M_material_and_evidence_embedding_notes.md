## Material Placement

Materials are not listed only at the top. They also appear at the point of use:

```text
教学准备：lists common stationery, low-threshold materials, tools, substitute materials.
观察发现：uses stationery structure image and learning sheet.
1+1 小设计：uses one material friend to solve one problem.
1+n 大变身：uses paper, thread, shaped materials, sticking, wrapping, folding, combining.
评价与证据：uses worksheet, photos, sketches, peer comments.
```

## Screen / PPT Placement

The draft names three screen functions:

```text
1. 文具结构观察图；
2. 问题和改进示例；
3. 材料和技法菜单。
```

These are embedded in the corresponding teaching process instead of becoming a
separate resource panel.

## Evidence Chain

```text
发现问题
→ 学习单记录
→ 1+1 小设计照片或草图
→ 1+n 改进作品
→ 材料技法选择理由
→ 同伴评价语
→ 后续购买建议书 / 课堂使用指南
```

## Teacher Confirmation

The draft preserves material uncertainty as teacher-confirmation points:

```text
学校材料是否充足；
是否允许改造真实文具；
是否需要纸质模型替代；
是否需要拍照保存过程证据。
```
